Benjamin Wagner

IT Contractor, Hobbyist Programmer


I am a self-taught programmer/computer technician. I got my start around 8 years old when I would make command prompt games in C++ with the help of programming books my dad had lying around. Since then, computers have been my biggest passion (alongside Jazz music). Over the years since, I have worked to develop my skills through various endeavors such as programming autonomous robots for my high school robotics team, participating in Ludum Dare game development competitions, freelance web development in College, CTF's and a multitude of personal projects.

I am dedicated to the lifelong student ethos and therefore, learning and continually sharpening my skills has and will always be my top priority.
